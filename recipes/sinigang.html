Sinigang

Description

Pork Sinigang is a delicious Filipino sour soup dish. The soup is made from any cut of pork along tomato, string beans, spinach, and tamarind.

This dish yields 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1(1/2 inch)piece fresh ginger, chopped
  • 2 plum tomatoes, cut into 1/2-inch dice
  • 1 lb bone-in pork chops
  • 4 cups water, more if needed
  • 1 (1.41 oz) package tamarind soup base mix
  • 1/2 lb fresh green beans, trimmed

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over meduim heat.
  2. Stir in the onion; cook and stir until the onion has softened and turned translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Season with salt.
  4. Stir in the ginger, tomatoes, and pork chops. Cover and reduce to medium-low.
  5. Turn the pork occasionally, until browned.
  6. Pour in the water and tamarind soup base.
  7. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Continue simmering until the pork is tender and cooked through, about 30 minutes.
  8. Stir in green beans and cook until tender.
